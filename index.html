Final Project: Squirrels in Central Park

Squirrels are cute, squirrels are fun!

How many squirrels do you think live in Central Park?


But how did we know that?

In October 2018, 323 squirrel sighters collected data on the squirrels of Central Park. Due to a reporter’s curiosity on these small furry friends, these individuals set out to count and tell the story of every squirrel they saw.

Before 1877, there were no squirrels in Central Park. However, they released 60-70 squirrels from 1877 to 1878 and let them roam around.
By 1883, there were 1,500 squirrels calling Central Park home. Now the park hosts 2,373 squirrels.

Let's test your knowledge on Squirrels!
